Island Flavors Meets Marina Views

Nestled on the waterfront in West End, Bahamas, Dockside Grill is where both locals and travelers come to gather. We focus on celebrating the bounty of our local oceans with simple, perfectly crafted dishes.

Whether you are stopping by boat after a day on the waters or staying for a week, our friendly team makes you feel right at home. From our legendary service to our iconic Bahamian recipes, every detail is crafted to perfection.
